WHY THIS MATTERS NOW

Closer than the calendar makes it feel.

The deadline is firm. From September 2026 every French business must be able to receive structured e-invoices, and large senders must issue them in the approved formats.

For SaaS billing this is harder than it is for a traditional business. Dynamic pricing, proration, multi-currency, and automatic renewals all have to map onto a rigid government format, on time, every cycle.

Getting it wrong is not only a fines problem. Rejected invoices mean delayed revenue, manual rework, and customers who cannot reclaim their VAT.
